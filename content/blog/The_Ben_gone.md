## (*Eng*) The Ben gone
The thing made me was me

You took it from me,

Remaing, is me.

This is disparate, not me..

## (*Tr*) Giden Ben
---
Beni ben yapan bendim.

O beni benden aldınız,

Kalan, benim.

Bu ben bambaşka, ben değil..
